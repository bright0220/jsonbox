Frequently Asked Questions

What is jsonbox.io?

jsonbox.io lets you store, read & modify JSON data over HTTP APIs for free.

Who can use it?

Its open for everyone to use it, but its ideal of small projects, prototypes or hackathons, where you don't have to spin up your own datastore.

How do we get started?

Copy the URL from the top and start sending HTTP requests to play around with your datastore. Please read the docs for more info.

Is it FREE?

The service is completely FREE to use for everyone. Although by default the boxes doesn't have any authentication. The data stored is as secure as the box id you use. You can also pay a nominal fee of $2/mon for private boxes, all requests to private boxes has to be authenticated using API-SECRET.

Click here to get a private box.

Is there any limits?

Yes. For a free box the size of a single record can't be more than 10KB. If you are using a private box, you can store upto 100KB of JSON data in a single record. But there is no limit to the number of records in both free & private boxes.

What is a box?

A box is a virtual entity where you put all your data. It is mandate to be present in all HTTP requests you send.

Where is our data stored?

Your data is securely stored in our MongoDb instances.

Do you sell our data to third parties?

No. Never. We don't sell your data to any third parties.

Can I download my data?

Of course. Please contact us for that.

How do we contact you?

You can contact us for any queries at jsonbox.io@gmail.com. We will respond within 24 to 48 hours.
